Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs contribution to the computer world was founding Apple, Pixar and NeXT. Apple is the biggest company that sells phones, ipads, macbooks, etc. Steve Jobs and his co-founders are mainly known for being the masterminds behind all the apple devices and for making history when they created the first iphone. They are the reasons we can text and communicate through devices faster. Something Jobs did that many people don't know is that he saved Apple from going broke. When Apple was in a finacial problem Jobs made the decision to cut the company's research lab.
